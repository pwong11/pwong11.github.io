About SmartDart

It can be challenging to learn how to play darts and throw them accurately. Our adaptive dartboard gradually adapts over time in difficulty.

SmartDart detects how well the player is throwing darts and adjusts the difficulty by changing the target size and distance

Proejct Overview

There are three different target sizes: 4", 8" and 12" diameters. The entire upper platform sits on a timing belt and idler pulley system that adjusts the target distance from the user. In each round, the user will throw 10 darts and push a button to indicate that the round is complete.
